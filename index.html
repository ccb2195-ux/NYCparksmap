Parks for Playing and Parks for Laying

NYC Parks keeps a detailed list of every park in the city, and what it is for. Have you ever thought to yourself, "I'd really love to go relax in the park today, but I don't know if I should play baseball instead?"

Well now you no longer have to worry. With this handy map, you can see which parks are for playing (Active) and which parks are for Laying (Passive) according to the Parks department. Also, find parks you didn't know existed! Click on them, visit them, touch grass!
